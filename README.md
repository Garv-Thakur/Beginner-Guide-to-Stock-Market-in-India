Beginner Guide to Stock Market in India

The stock market in India is one of the most effective ways of creating wealth. If you are either a complete newbie or planning to initiate your investment journey, then it is extremely important that you understand the basic concepts of the stock market. In this guide, we will be covering everything from basic concepts to smart trading techniques.

**What is the Stock Market?**

The stock market is where companies go to raise capital by issuing shares, which are then bought by investors, making them part owners of that particular company. There are two main stock exchanges in India. They are:

**National Stock Exchange (NSE)**
**Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE)**

Every day, millions of transactions occur in this market, making it a highly dynamic platform for investment.

**How Does the Stock Market Work?**

The stock market is based on the concept of supply and demand. When there is more demand for a particular stock, its value rises. However, if there is more supply than demand, then the value of that particular stock drops.

To trade in the stock market, you need a broker. Popular options in India include:

Zerodha
Angel One
Upstox

**Types of Trading in India**

1. Intraday Trading

Buying and selling stocks on the same day.

2. Swing Trading

Holding stocks for a few days or weeks to benefit from short-term trends.

3. Long-Term Investing

Investing for months or years to grow wealth steadily.
